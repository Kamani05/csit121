Chef's Choice Pasta

Delicious Homemade Pasta

Recipe by: Amani Ngomasele

Ingredients

  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 14.5 ounces tomato sauce
  • 100 grams of angel hair pasta
  • Ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Bring a medium pot of salted water to a boil, add pasta and cook for 5 minutes.
  2. While pasta cooks, fry the ground beef until well done.
  3. Remove from the heat, and set aside.
  4. When the pasta is cooked, drain it, reserving half a cup of pasta cooking water.
  5. Immediately return the hot, drained pasta to the pot.
  6. Pour the tomato sauce and ground beef mixture over the pasta. Let it cook for 15 minutes.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
